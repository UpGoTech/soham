Retire to comfort, security and graceful living in the heart of Nagpur.

SOHAM – where ageing can be the start of a new carefree chapter in your life.......

The first of its kind in Central India, Soham is a cluster of fully furnished studio apartments emphasising on providing a wholesome living environment to the elderly. It is more like a retirement resort than a senior living facility.

Rules & Regulations

Admission

A.   Eligibility for Admission

Admission is open to any senior citizen above the age of 65. Only persons who are in general good health and mobile will be eligible for admission.

B.   Ineligibility for Admission

Senior citizen who are suffering from infectious diseases or mental illness or are involved in criminal cases or offences or are addicted to liquor or other psychotropic substances will not be granted admission.

Medical screening by a doctor on the Trust’s panel shall be mandatory to be eligible.

The Trust reserves the right to follow any procedure it may deem fit to verify the credentials of the prospective resident.

Admission Procedure

1 . Any person desirous of becoming a resident of Soham will be admitted on the approval of the Board of Trustees.

2. Any person wishing to stay at ‘Soham’ shall apply on the prescribed form along with a declaration and affidavit agreeing to abide by the rules and regulations of the Trust.

3. Admission form should be accompanied by 2 recent photographs of the person seeking admission and the form should be duly signed by 1 guarantor along with undertakings from 1 close relative.

4. The mode of admission will be in accordance with the assessment reached by the authorities and on the basis of interview with the applicant.

5. Every person applying for admission shall be required to produce a consent letter and credentials from near relatives like children, brother, sister who have attained age of majority along with their contact details. If required, the relatives may be called for the interview along with applicant.

6. The applicant should also furnish the following documents
          » Proof of Age
          » Proof of Address
          » Full name and address of near relatives along with their relationship to them.
          » Details of spouse

7. A non-interest bearing security deposit will have to be deposited with ‘Soham ‘. This deposit will be refunded after adjustment of dues when the resident leaves ‘Soham’.

8. The management shall have the sole discretion to accept or reject any application made by any individual without assigning any reasons. The decision of the management shall be final and binding.

Termination of Admission

Admission, if granted may be terminated with 15 days’ notice if it is found that the resident has obtained admission by means of false representation or who may have indulged in acts of indiscipline/ misconduct.

Soham reserves the right to ask a resident to leave for not abiding by the Rules and Regulations or for ill treating co-residents, spreading negativity and creating an unhealthy atmosphere.

No applicant will be re-admitted once they have been asked to leave from ‘Soham’.

The decision of the Trustees shall be final and binding.

Accomadation

1. Single bedded rooms with attached bath and toilet with grab bars, equipped with an A/C, Fridge, 32 in LED TV, cupboard, bed with bedside table and a chair with a writing desk. (225 sq ft)

2. Double bedded room with all of the above facilities with an additional cupboard and chair. (300 sq ft)

3. Fixed set channels will be provided for TV.

4. No cooking shall be permitted inside the rooms.

5. All valuables should be kept in the cupboard under lock and key at the risk of resident.

6. Resident may only bring their clothes, personal toiletries and towels etc. with them. No item of furniture will be allowed.

7. No resident shall be allowed to run a private business from the premises of Soham.

8. The Trust shall make arrangement for once a day cleaning of the room and toilet.

9. All property of the Trust should be used properly and any damage done to it shall be recovered from the resident.

10. No clothes or foot wear of the resident or their visitors will be allowed to be placed outside their room and the corridor should be clear at all times.

11. No article on the premises which includes room and common area shall be moved without the consent of the management.

12. Room service may only be requested if the resident is temporarily ill.

Visitors & Movement of Residents

1. To maintain continuous contact of residents with family and friends, visitors will be allowed from 10 am to 7 pm. Tea/coffee/snacks for the guest may be ordered from the kitchen for a specified charge subject to availability.

2. Any resident going out of Soham should inform the front office and return by 8 pm except in case of an emergency or with the prior permission of the management.

3. Any resident wishing to go and stay out of Soham may do so only after recording the prescribed details in the Register kept with the manager.

4. There shall be no reduction in the monthly charge even if the resident has gone for a holiday.

5. If a resident is not contactable after leaving Soham for more than the time he/she has informed, the management reserves the right to contact the local guardian for removal of their personal belongings from their room and the room may be allotted to the person next on the waiting list.

Death or Funeral

In the unfortunate event of the death of a resident, all efforts shall be made by the Trust to contact his/her relative and local guardian as soon as possible on telephone as well as at the address given in our records. In case either of them fail to satisfactorily respond in a timely manner or fail to take possession of the body within 8 hours of the demise, the Trust shall perform the funeral rites in such a manner as may be appropriate. If for any reason beyond our control, we are unable to contact either the relative or guardian whose contact details are on our records, the above action shall be taken only after a period of 8 hours.

No complaint or grievance whatsoever as regards the medical treatment or alleged negligence of any sort and any issues as regards the disposal of the body will be entertained.

The address and telephone numbers of the nearest relative to be intimated in the event of serious illness or death of the resident should be given at the time of admission itself.

If any resident desires to donate his/her body parts such as eyes, kidney etc. or his/her body or death, the same should be declared beforehand and a copy of a signed affidavit should be given to the Trust so that we may be able to take appropriate action in time to carry out the wishes of the deceased resident.

The belongings of the deceased resident should be collected by his/her guardian or children within a period of 15 days. The Trust reserves the right to dispose them off if they are not collected in the mentioned time.

Code of Conduct

1. The resident should live in a cordial and friendly manner with co-residents and staff members.

2. The resident should respect the rights and privacy of other residents and conduct himself/herself in such a manner as to maintain peace and harmony at all times.

3. All the residents are expected to be punctual as regards the times prescribed for their meals in the Dining Room.

4. Drug abuse, intoxicants, alcohol abuse or any kind of illegal activity is strictly prohibited at Soham. Smoking is discouraged but a resident may smoke only in the open area or terrace. If any resident in close proximity objects, then the smoker shall respect that and move away.

5. Electricity and Water are scarce resources and residents are advised to use them judiciously. If a resident consistently keeps appliances on when not in the room extra charges will be levied at the discretion of the management.

Financials

1. The resident/ guarantor shall pay the monthly charges within the last 7 days of the preceding month for which it is due (i.e. the monthly charges should be paid between January 25th and 31st January for the month of February)
Note: Soham reserves the right to make changes to its monthly charges and adequate prior notice shall be served upon the resident before the increase.

2. If the resident/guarantor fails to pay monthly charges before the required date, a 20% late fee shall be applicable till a maximum period of 2 months. If the resident/ guarantor fails to make the due payments along with the late fee within the period of 2 months, the right of the resident to stay in Soham shall forthwith cease and the resident shall vacate the allotted room. If the resident fails to vacate and/or the guarantor does not take custody, the Management representative shall enter and remove the belongings of the resident and may shift the resident to a destitutes dormitory.

3. The Management has the absolute discretion to enhance the monthly charges. However, the same would be decided in the month of October of any year and will come into effect on the 1st of January of the succeeding year. As far as Security Deposit is concerned, it shall not change for the existing resident who have already deposited it. However, the same may be enhanced for new admissions.

Disclaimer

The Management of Soham shall not be responsible for the loss of any personal article of the residents or in case of any unfortunate accident on the premises of Soham.
