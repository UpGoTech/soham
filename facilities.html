Retire to comfort, security and graceful living in the heart of Nagpur.

SOHAM – where ageing can be the start of a new carefree chapter in your life.......

The first of its kind in Central India, Soham is a cluster of fully furnished studio apartments emphasising on providing a wholesome living environment to the elderly. It is more like a retirement resort than a senior living facility.

Facilities

Due care has been taken to make your stay comfortable.

Right from tea or coffee in the morning to dinner at night, we will be taking care of meals, health, entertainment, sleep-cycle, hygiene, and everything else there is.

Here’s a comprehensive list of our facilities:

1) Meals and Snacks – Morning bevarage, breakfast, lunch, afternoon bevarage with snacks and dinner

2) Bedroom – Bed, mattress, pillow, pillow cover, bed linen, bed cover, quilt, curtains, cupboard with mirror, chair, side table, writing desk, chair, fridge, AC and a TV + 24/7 running hot water

3) Health – Regular visits by highly qualified Doctors whose visiting hours shall be routinely notified, 24/7 Nurses for checking Blood Pressure, Sugar Levels, etc. and to assist with medication prescribed by the Doctor. We also have a well-equipped physiotherapy center.

4) Fitness – Indoor Gym, garden gym and a walking track

5) Community Bonding – Library, common room with indoor games and a TV

6) Medical Emergency – Soham shall make necessary arrangements to shift a member to the nearest hospital of their choice. (Expenses of the hospital and medication thereof to be borne by the member or the local guardian of the resident)

7) Hygiene – Cleaning of toilets (Daily), changing of bed linen (Twice a week) and laundry facility on payment, self-use of washing machine in designated area on nominal charge per load.

8) High speed wireless internet with maximum range for free internet surfing

9) Parking Facility, as per availability on monthly payment
